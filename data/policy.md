# Travel Reimbursement Policy

## Hotel
- Maximum reimbursement: ₹5000 per night
- Receipt is mandatory.

## Meals
- Maximum reimbursement: ₹1500 per day
- Receipt required if amount exceeds ₹500.

## Taxi
- Maximum reimbursement: ₹1000 per day.
- Receipt recommended.

## Flight
- Economy class only.
- Business class requires Director approval.

## Approval Rules
- Claims above ₹20,000 require Director approval.

## General Rules
- Duplicate claims are not allowed.
- Missing mandatory receipts require Manual Review.
